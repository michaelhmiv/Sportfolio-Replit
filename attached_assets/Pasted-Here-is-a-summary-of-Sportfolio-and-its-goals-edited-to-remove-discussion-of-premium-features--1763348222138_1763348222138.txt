### Key Things to Know as a New User

* **It's a Virtual Economy:** Sportfolio is a game based on virtual currency. There are **no cash-out options**.
* **Persistence is Key:** Unlike seasonal fantasy, you don't have to redraft your players every year. If you mine a player and they become a star, you can benefit from that investment for their *entire career*.
* **You Control the Market:** A player's mining rate is the same for everyone (100 shares/hour). Their value is only determined by what other users are willing to pay for them on the Exchange, based on their contest performance or hype.

In short, your goal is to **Mine** shares of players you believe in, and then decide whether to **Trade** them for profit or **Compete** with them in contests to win prizes.
